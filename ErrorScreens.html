Payment unsuccessful

Your payment didn't go through.

Don't worry — this happens sometimes and it's easily fixed. Your appointment time is still being held for you for the next 10 minutes while you try again.

What we know

Your questionnaire responses and tongue photo are saved
Your selected appointment time is held for 10 minutes
No payment was taken — your card has not been charged
The most common reasons are an incorrect card number, an expired card, or insufficient funds. Check your details and try again.

If this keeps happening, please contact us and we'll get you booked in manually.

Time no longer available

That time was just taken.

Someone else booked that appointment at the same moment. This doesn't happen often, and we're sorry for the inconvenience. Your payment has been fully refunded.

What we've done

Your payment has been fully refunded — no charge was made
Your questionnaire responses and tongue photo are saved
The time you selected is no longer available

Please choose another available time. Refunds typically appear within 3–5 business days depending on your bank.

If you'd prefer us to book you in directly, please call us.

Photo not uploaded

We couldn't save your photo.

Something went wrong when uploading your tongue photo. This is usually a connection issue or an unsupported file type. Your progress is saved — just try again.

Common causes

Slow or interrupted internet connection during upload
File type not supported — we accept JPEG and PNG only
File size too large — please keep photos under 10MB
Your questionnaire responses are saved. You only need to retake or re-upload the photo — you won't need to fill in the questionnaire again.

Still having trouble? Contact us and we can arrange an alternative way to share your photo.

For your security, we sign you out after a period of inactivity. Your questionnaire responses have been saved — just sign back in to pick up where you left off.

What's been saved

Your questionnaire responses are saved on this device
Your tongue photo is saved if the upload completed
Your account and formula history are safe
