This Privacy Policy explains how Justin Keane Chinese Medicine ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores and protects your personal information when you use the Justin Keane Chinese Medicine Women's Health Platform ("the Platform").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information — particularly your sensitive health information — with care, transparency and in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), and the Health Records Act 2001 (Vic).

By using the Platform and ticking the consent checkbox before beginning the questionnaire, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

02

What information we collect

We collect two categories of information:

Personal information
Your name, email address, mobile number and state of residence — collected when you create an account and complete your booking details.
Sensitive health information
Your age, menstrual cycle status, health concerns, energy patterns, temperature tendencies and any additional health notes you provide — collected through the intake questionnaire.

A photograph of the surface of your tongue — collected as a clinical diagnostic tool prior to your consultation.

Your consultation history and any herbal formulas prescribed to you.
Payment information
Your payment is processed by Stripe. We do not collect or store your credit card details. Stripe handles all card data in accordance with PCI DSS standards.
Your sensitive health information is classified as sensitive information under the Privacy Act. We collect it only with your explicit consent and only for the purpose of providing you with clinical care.

04

How we use your information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

Your questionnaire responses and tongue photograph are reviewed by Dr Keane before your phone consultation to inform his clinical assessment and formula recommendation.
To manage your booking

We will never use your information for marketing purposes without your separate, explicit consent. We will never sell your information to any third party.

06

Your tongue photograph

Your tongue photograph is a clinical diagnostic tool used in Chinese Medicine to assess your internal health state. We treat it with the same care as any other sensitive health information.

How your tongue photograph is handled:

It is uploaded securely to a private, encrypted storage location in Australia (AWS S3, Sydney region). It is accessible only to Dr Justin Keane for the purpose of your clinical consultation. It is attached to your patient record in Cliniko and labelled with your name and the date of your consultation. It is never shared with any third party other than Cliniko. It is never used for any purpose other than your clinical care. It is never made publicly accessible.

Your tongue photograph will be retained for 7 years from the date of your consultation in accordance with health record-keeping requirements under the Health Records Act 2001 (Vic). You may request earlier deletion at any time by contacting us — see Section 9.

07

How we store and protect your information

We take the security of your personal and health information seriously. The following measures are in place:

Encryption
All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit). Tongue photographs are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption. Your account password is hashed and never stored in plain text.
Australian data storage
All your personal and health information is stored in Australia. Tongue photographs are stored in AWS Sydney region (ap-southeast-2). Patient records are stored in Cliniko's Australian servers.
Access controls
Access to your health information is restricted to Dr Justin Keane. Tongue photographs are accessible only via secure, time-limited links. No public access is permitted to any health data.
Payment security
Card details are handled entirely by Stripe and never pass through our systems. We are out of scope for PCI DSS when Stripe Elements is used correctly.

In the unlikely event of a data breach that is likely to result in serious harm, we will notify affected individuals and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) as required under the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.

09

Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act and the Health Records Act, you have the following rights:

Access
You have the right to request access to the personal and health information we hold about you. We will respond within 30 days.
Correction
If you believe any information we hold about you is inaccurate, incomplete or out of date, you may request that we correct it.
Deletion
You may request deletion of your tongue photographs at any time. Deletion of clinical records may be subject to our legal retention obligations under the Health Records Act 2001 (Vic).
Withdrawal of consent
You may withdraw your consent to the collection of your health information at any time by contacting us. Please note that withdrawal of consent may prevent us from providing ongoing clinical care.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 12. We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days and respond in full within 30 days.
