03

The nature of this service

Important — please read carefully.

The intake questionnaire and tongue photograph are triage and intake tools only. They are not a clinical consultation and do not constitute medical advice. No diagnosis is made and no formula is issued on the basis of the questionnaire or photograph alone.

The legal and clinical consultation is the real-time phone call with Dr Justin Keane. Your herbal formula is issued by Dr Keane personally, following that call, based on his clinical judgement.

This service is a complementary health service. It is not a substitute for medical advice, diagnosis or treatment by a medical doctor. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, call 000 immediately.

If Dr Keane determines on the phone call that your presentation is more complex than the Platform is designed to serve, he may redirect you to an in-person consultation. In this circumstance your consultation fee will be refunded in full.

Chinese herbal medicine is a regulated health profession in Australia. Dr Justin Keane is registered with AHPRA and practises in accordance with the Chinese Medicine Board of Australia's standards for telehealth and virtual care.

04

Your consultation

Your phone consultation with Dr Keane will take approximately 10 minutes. To make the most of your consultation:

Be available at your booked time
Dr Keane will call the mobile number you provided at your booked time. Please ensure you are in a quiet location and able to speak freely about your health.
Disclose all relevant health information
Please disclose all current medications, supplements, diagnosed conditions and relevant health history to Dr Keane on the call. Withholding relevant information may affect the safety and appropriateness of your formula.
Tongue photo accuracy
Please follow the tongue photo instructions carefully. Your photo must be taken before tongue scraping, at least 30 minutes after eating or drinking, in natural light, showing the full surface of your tongue from root to tip. Inaccurate photos may affect the clinical assessment.

Dr Keane will record brief consultation notes following your call. These notes, along with your questionnaire responses and tongue photograph, form part of your clinical record and are stored in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
